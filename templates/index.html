How to use SACA

In just a few taps we'll help you figure out the next best step.

  1. Share how you feel. Tap Tap to speak or Write your symptoms to tell us what’s going on.
  2. Answer quick follow-ups. A couple of short questions help us understand the bigger picture.
  3. Review personalised advice. We’ll suggest what to do next and highlight anything urgent.
